Two WWE NXT Stars Set To Compete At 9/23 Reality Of Wrestling Event

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, May 29, 2024

WWE NXT is considered to be the developmental brand of the Stamford-based promotion. The Tuesday Night show features a plethora of talent. The General Manager of the show is none other than Hall of Famer Shawn Michaels.

It was recently announced that two top stars from the Stamford-based promotion will be wrestling each other at Booker T’s Reality of Wrestling. The promotion is set to host an event later this month on the 23rd. The show will be taking place in Texas City, Texas. The event is titled The Next Chapter.

The two NXT wrestlers taking part in the show are Axiom and Charlie Dempsey.

Background

Axiom is a Spanish-born wrestler. He initially showed up on the Stamford-based promotion under the name A-Kid. He competed first in NXT UK. The 26-year-old star also won the first-ever United Kingdom Heritage Cup.

Charlie Dempsey on the other hand is the son of WWE veteran William Regal. He made his WWE debut back in 2021 under the ring name Bailey Matthews against Tyler Bate. Dempsey has partnered up with former Cruiserweight Champion Drew Gulak.

Recently, Reality of Wrestling broke the news that Axiom and Dempsey will be wrestling each other at the Next Chapter event.

“BREAKING NEWS Reality of Wrestling’s taking things to the NEXT Level with our #GrandOpening event on Sat, Sept 23rd bringing our fans incredible match ups they can’t see anywhere else. — NXT SHOWCASE MATCH — @Axiom_WWE vs Charlie Dempsey,” Reality of Wrestling tweeted.

Charlie Dempsey and Axiom had previously competed on NXT UK. They have also competed in multiple house shows.
