In the past, Jim Jefferies and Kate Luyben were a couple.

Jefferies and Kate Luyben met while filming the pilot for Jefferies’ 2013 TV series Legit in 2011. Kate portrayed a prostitute in the pilot. In a short period, the pair became excellent friends and began dating. The couple became pregnant with their first kid just months after they began dating.

Jefferies and Luyben welcomed their baby, Hank, on November 7, 2012. At the premiere of Legit, Jefferies disclosed that Luyben was his girlfriend and they had recently had a kid together a few months after the birth of their child.

Jefferies and Kate Luyben split up shortly after. Even after their divorce, the couple maintained a cordial relationship and raised their child together.

Did Jim have a relationship with Ginger Gonzaga?

Although numerous websites claim Jim Jefferies dated actress Ginger Gonzaga, there is yet to be any proof of their relationship.

Gonzaga, 37, is an American actress and comedian best known for her role in the 2019 series Living with Yourself, in which she co-starred with Paul Rudd.

The putative pair appeared in Jim’s 2016 film Punching Henry. Following that, Jim uploaded a handful of images with the actress on social media, leading some of his followers to assume they were dating.

Meanwhile, Gonzaga was dating another famous comic, Jim Carrey. Carrey, 58, a Canadian-American actor and comedian, and Gonzaga dated for many months before splitting up in October 2019.
