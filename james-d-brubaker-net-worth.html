James D. Brubaker (born March 30, 1937) is an American film producer, production manager, and film actor.Brubaker was born in Hollywood, Los Angeles in the United States. Brubaker is of Russian and Scottish descent.Brubaker had won the 1999 Directors Guild of America Award (DGA) for the film Gia.Brubaker was nominated for the 1998 Primetime Emmy Award for the television movie Gia.He is also credited as James Brubaker or Jim Brubaker. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
