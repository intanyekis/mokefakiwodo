Britney Spears References Ex-Boyfriend Justin Timberlake In A Bizarre New Instagram Post

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Highlights

  • Key Takeaways:
  • Britney Spears caused excitement by posting a video dancing to Justin Timberlake's song "Holy Grail" on Instagram in a neon green mini dress and chunky jewelry.
  • Britney changed her hairstyle throughout the clip and seemed to reciprocate eye contact with someone off-camera.
  • Justin Timberlake previously implied that Britney cheated on him but later apologized, and he is now trying to prevent her from discussing their relationship in her upcoming memoir.

Britney Spears sent fans into a frenzy on Tuesday when she posted a video clip dancing to her ex-boyfriend Justin Timberlake’s song “Holy Grail,” which also features Jay-Z.

Wearing a neon green mini dress, brown high heels and chunky jewelry, Britney showed off a series of dance moves in the video, which she then posted to Instagram.

Throughout the clip, Britney changes her hairstyle, going from wearing it down to up. Someone featured off-camera waved their hand towards Britney, while she appeared to reciprocate their eye contact.

“I put on my party dress!!!!!! My first yellow tutu !!!!” the pop star captioned the video.

Justin Did Britney Wrong After Their Break-Up, But Later Apologized

It’s been over a decade since Britney and Justin broke up, but fans are still eager for more details of their high-profile romance. The pair dated from 1999 to 2002, before a messy break-up.

Justin seemed to imply that Britney cheated on him when he cast a look-a-like in his music video for “Cry Me A River,” released shortly after their break-up. Justin later went on to apologize in 2021 or how he treated Britney post-split.

In the same post, he also apologized to Janet Jackson for failing to support her after she had a wardrobe malfunction during their joint Super Bowl performance.

"I specifically want to apologise to Britney Spears and Janet Jackson both individually, because I care for and respect these women and I know I failed,” he wrote. “I also feel compelled to respond, in part, because everyone involved deserves better and most importantly, because this is a larger conversation that I wholeheartedly want to be part of and grow from.”

"The industry is flawed. It sets men, especially white men, up for success,” Justin continued. “It's designed this way. As a man in a privileged position I have to be vocal about this.”

Justin Is Trying To Stop Britney From Talking About Him In Her Memoir

Last year, it was confirmed that Britney is releasing a memoir, after she signed a $15 million deal with publishing house Simon & Schuster. Not only is she expected to discuss her conservatorship and family, but fans are hoping she’ll discuss her infamous relationship with Justin Timberlake.

According to a recent report by Daily Mail, attorneys for Justin as well as one of Britney’s other exes, Collin Farrell, have filed a motion to delay the memoir’s publication for at least four months. Apparently, both actors want certain sections of the book omitted.

“Lawyers demanded to see her book in advance and were adamant that some of the revelations were removed,” a source claimed. “'There are still plenty of gobsmacking tales but Justin and Colin were conscious about what could be said about them.”

However, another source told the publication that there have been “no legal threats from Justin or Colin.” Britney herself has not confirmed or denied the speculation.

The singer has been vocal about how she hopes she can share her personal experiences to help others in similar positions. "I'm here to be an advocate for people with real disabilities and real illnesses," she previously said. "Hopefully, my story will make an impact and make some changes in the corrupt system."

Britney’s memoir is still expected to be released sometime this year.
