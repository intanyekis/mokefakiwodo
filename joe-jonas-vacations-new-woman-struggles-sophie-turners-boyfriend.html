Joe Jonas Vacations With New Woman As He Struggles With Sophie Turners Boyfriend

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 15, 2024

Highlights

  • Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner are both moving on from their relationship, as Joe was seen on vacation with model Stormi Bree and Sophie has been seen with British aristocrat Peregrine Pearson.
  • Joe and Stormi's relationship status remains unclear, as they kept PDA to a minimum and have not publicly acknowledged their connection.
  • Joe is reportedly frustrated and concerned about Sophie's actions, including public hang-outs with his ex Taylor Swift, and how they may affect their daughters.

Although Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner are still in the process of finalizing their divorce, the former couple appear to have both moved on with other people. Weeks after Sophie was spotted spending time with a British socialite, Joe started the new year with a vacation alongside a new woman.

Joe Went To Mexico With Stormi Bree

TMZ recently published photos of Joe in Los Cabos alongside Stormi Bree, a model and a former Miss Teen USA. It appears the duo spent the new year celebrations in the Mexican resort destination, but it was coming to an end, as they were seen boarding a private jet at the airport.

Joe and Stormi kept the PDA to a minimum, so the status of their relationship (if there is one, that is) remains up in the air. But they coordinated in all-black looks. Stormi wore dark jeans and a long sleeve t-shirt, while Joe rocked a black hoodie with a bright red hat and sunglasses.

TMZ notes this is the first time Joe and Sophie have been seen together in public. They also don’t appear to be following each other on social media, suggesting their romance may be new and/or they’re trying to maintain a low-profile.

It's likely Joe is returning to the U.S. as his daughters are set to return to him after spending the holidays in the U.K. with their mother Sophie Turner. The exes reached a temporary custody agreement in October, after Sophie accused Joe of kidnapping their children when he refused to allow them to go to Britain with Sophie, even though they were apparently planning to move their before their split.

Sophie Is Dating A British Aristocrat

It's not only Joe who’s moving on from their relationship. Sophie has also been seen with a new man, British aristocrat Peregrine Pearson. The pair were first romantically connected in October.

Initial reports said Joe was struggling to see his estranged wife move on so quickly, even though he was the one who initiated the divorce (which Sophie has said took her by surprise).

“Joe is livid with the latest coming from Sophie, making him look like a horrible father,” an insider claimed to Daily Mail. “'It is disgusting in his eyes what she has chosen to do.”

“This is only going to be the start of stuff getting messier and messier and messier,” they continued. “Joe thought they were moving in a forward direction with the divorce and finding a way to co-parent but now it is just a nasty mess, and the latest lawsuit threw any type of positivity all off course.”

Joe took Sophie to a show of Taylor's, and it wasn't even comped. Again, he is who filed for divorce. Read between the line.”
