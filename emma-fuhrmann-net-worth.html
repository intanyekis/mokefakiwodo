Emma Fuhrmann Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Emma Cate Fuhrmann (born September 15, 2001) is an American actress who has been acting since she was 5 years old. She was first noticed for her role in "The Magic of Belle Isle" (2012) as Finnegan O'Neil. This was her first lead role in a motion picture playing opposite Morgan Freeman and Virginia Madsen. The film was directed by Rob Reiner. She co-starred in the Warner Brothers film Blended (2014), starring Adam Sandler and Drew Barrymore and directed by Frank Coraci. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
