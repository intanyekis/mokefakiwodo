Billy Bob Thornton Bio Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

‘The Big Bang Theory’ may be a comedy, but that certainly doesn’t stop the show from taking on some serious topics — such as feminism. On the Oct. 30, episode, Penny, Amy and Bernadette discuss female sexuality in the workplace — and of course they have very different viewpoints on the subject!
The Big Bang Theory returned to Thursday nights on Oct. 30, with a new episode, “The Misinterpretation Agitation.” Billy Bob Thornton, 59, guest starred as creepy Dr. Lorvis who professed his love to Penny (Kaley Cuoco) while Amy (Mayim Bialik) and Brenadette (Melissa Rauch) clash over if women can be both sexy and smart.
