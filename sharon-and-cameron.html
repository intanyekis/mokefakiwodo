Sharon and cameron - The Young and the Restless

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

I’m adding some details because lots of viewers weren’t watching 20 years ago. And they covered a lot but not all the details today.

Sharon and Nick met with Chance today and go over Sharon’s backstory with Cameron including Flashbacks.

Sharon met Cameron in 2003 during a time she and Nick were having trouble in their marriage. Cameron met her in a bar and they had a consensual romantic encounter. But Cameron was obsessed with Sharon and abusive. He refused to let her leave and she snuck out later.

A year later when Sharon was back home and reunited with Nick. Cameron showed up in Genoa City to do business with Newman Enterprises. He taunted Sharon and threatened to expose their past in Denver to Nick. Following a New Year’s Eve Party Cameron blackmailed Sharon into showing up at a motel.

When she arrived at the motel, she rejected Cameron’s advances, which sent him into a fit of rage. He started attacking her and attempted to force himself on her. She was only able to escape by hitting him over the head with a champagne bottle, knocking him unconscious. Assuming he was dead, Sharon dragged his body to a nearby alley.

He wasn’t dead and plotted revenge. At first, he pretended to be a ghost and haunted Sharon, driving her mad. He then killed Sharon’s old boyfriend, Cassie and Mariah’s (Camryn Grimes) father, and framed Sharon for the murder. Once Cameron finally revealed himself to be alive, he convinced Sharon the only way to keep her out of prison for the murder was for her to run away with him. Sharon reluctantly agreed to leave town with him.

However, while on the plane, Cameron’s plans to keep Sharon around forever were ruined when Nick surprised him and was on the plane and knocked out Cameron. Nick then strapped a parachute on Cameron and pushed him out of the aircraft. Nick and Sharon jumped out of the plane following him, and all three landed in a cornfield.

They walked through the farmland until they found a farmer and Nick and Sharon were able to call the police. Cameron had a broken leg so he wasn’t able to walk well. Cameron was last seen in 2004 being carted off to prison.

We know from Chance that Cameron had a girlfriend after prison that looked like Sharon. He beat her up and came to GC before he could be arrested again.
