Bob Peterson Biography

Bob Peterson is an American animator, screenwriter, storyboard supervisor, director, as well as a voice actor who works at Pixar, he was born on January 18, 1961, in Ohio, United States as Robert Peterson. Bob was hired at Pixar by Roger Gould in 1994 as an animator for commercials, before subsequently becoming an animator for Toy Story (1995).

Bob was the co-director for Up (2009), in which he also voiced the characters Dug and Alpha, he was a co-writer on Finding Nemo (2003) and Cars 3 (2017).
