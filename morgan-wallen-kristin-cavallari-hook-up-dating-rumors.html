Did Morgan Wallen Hook Up With Kristin Cavallari? The Truth About Their Wild Dating Rumors

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, September 1, 2024

Highlights

  • Reality star Kristin Cavallari and country singer Morgan Wallen have been rumored to be dating, but there is no concrete evidence supporting this claim.
  • Both Cavallari and Wallen have had public relationships in the past, but there is no proof that they are currently dating each other or anyone else.
  • Cavallari has expressed that she is ready for a relationship after her divorce from Jay Cutler, but she is currently focused on her friends and kids and not actively searching for love.

Reality star Kristin Cavallari may have lived in California when she was younger and filming the drama-heavy series Laguna Beach and spin-off The Hills. But she has spent the last few years raising her family in kids in Franklin, Tennessee. According to People, she bought a house in 2020 when she was moving on from her ex-husband Jay Cutler. Given the time that Cavallari has spent in the southern state, it might not come as a total surprise that she has recently been linked to Morgan Wallen, who is known for his country music.

Kristin Cavallari and Morgan Wallen have each had some very public relationships, and since they have each been on the dating scene for the past few years, it's no wonder that people wonder when they will settle down. Here's the truth about the rumors that they are seeing each other.

Are Kristin Cavallari And Morgan Wallen Dating?

In June 2023, DeuxMoi posted about Kristin Cavallari and Morgan Wallen possibly being in a relationship. According to Aceshowbiz.com, when the Instagram account asked "Chaos theory... what are you guys talking about this week?" someone asked, "Morgan Wallen who is he dating???" DeuxMoi replied "Kristin Cavallari?"

Someone reached out to the DeuxMoi account and wrote, "Morgan Wallen and KCav spotted in Nash."

No one has any real and concrete details about Wallen and Cavallari, which suggests that this is just a rumor. There are a few TikTok posts about the stars seen in an elevator in a building in Chicago.

According to a post in the SubReddit CelebWivesofNashville, DeuxMoi then said that Cavallari was seeing another singer, Riley Green.

There is no proof that Kristin Cavallari is dating Riley Green, either, though.

Since DeuxMoi reports on tips and comments from fans of the Instagram account, they often share dating rumors that end up not being true. If Kristin Cavallari and Morgan Wallen did meet up in Nashville and go out on a date, there is no other information out there about it.

They haven't said that they were together, either. While it's still possible that the stars could confirm that they are dating and more details could be shared in the future, that might not end up happening.

Who Has Morgan Wallen Dated?

There were rumors that Morgan Wallen dated Olivia Dunne, but there doesn't appear to be any evidence that is true. The country singer has dated a few people, though. Wallen was in a relationship with Paige Lorenze and according to Page Six, they broke up in February 2022.

Before that, the singer was engaged to KT Smith. Us Weekly reported that they broke up in 2019 after two years together. In July 2020, Smith gave birth to their baby Indigo Wilder.

In February 2023, Wallen talked about being single in an interview with Country Countdown USA. He said, “Not right now, I don’t. I had a girlfriend for a while. Not recently. That was the last one I had. I don’t have anybody right now. It’s kind of hard."

Wallen explained the reason why it can be tough to date. He said, "Just how busy I’ve been. I got a son, I got an album, I got a tour, I’ve got all these things I do. I go hunt, it’s really hard for me to make time for that. I want to, though. I think I’m coming up on my ‘settle down phase.’ We’ll see. I’m not in a hurry.”

Since the singer made those comments just a few months ago, it's possible that he's still single and that his busy life is still making dating a major challenge.

Who Has Kristin Cavallari Dated?

It doesn't seem that Kristin Cavallari has had any major relationships since she and Jay Culter got divorced in 2020. Since they got married in 2013 and have two children, going through a divorce must have been really hard.

Us Weekly reported that Cavallari went out with Chase Rice in the summer of 2021. In 2020, she was dating Jeff Dye. Those relationships didn't last all that long, and it doesn't seem that she has been seeing anyone since.

In February 2023, people were certain that Cavallari was dating Tyler Cameron because they posed for pictures for her brand Uncommon James together. Since they kissed during the shoot, that got the rumor mill started. When Cameron spoke to E! News, he talked about her as a friend.

Since 2020, the reality star and designer has talked quite a bit about her love life.

According to Us Weekly, in April 2022, Cavallari shared some details on her Instagram Stories. She said that fans kept asking about whether she was seeing anyone. It sounds like she wanted to let her fans know that dealing with her divorce wasn't easy.

She said, “I’ve honestly needed the past 2 years to work through some heavy stuff. I dated a little here and there but no one serious. I am finally in a place where I’m ready for a relationship." She continued, I realize that didn’t really answer that question. Let’s just say this: when you are able to finally fully close a chapter, it’s amazing what you will start to attract.”

In June 2023, Kristin Cavallari explained that while she hasn't found love just yet, she has dated. She said, "I've gone a lot of dates in the last few years, but I don't like a lot of people and I don't [think I'm] picky. I just think it's because I don't have time for bulls--t anymore."

She also said that she is all about her friends and her kids rather than searching for love right now and explained, "It's really not important to me right now. It's just not with where I'm at in my life."

It doesn't seem like Kristin Cavallari and Morgan Wallen are dating. But while there may be no truth to these rumors, the stars have one thing in common: they seem to want to find someone to share their lives with.
