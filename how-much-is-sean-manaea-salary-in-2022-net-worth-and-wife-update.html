How Much Is Sean Manaea Salary? Net Worth And Wife Update

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, May 25, 2024

Sean Anthony Manaea is a Major League Baseball pitcher from the United States who currently pitches for the San Diego Padres of the MLB.

Sean Manaea was a professional baseball player for the Oakland Athletics and a college baseball player for Indiana State University. He was voted the best talent in the Cape Cod Baseball League in 2012 while at Indiana State.

Manaea was drafted 34th overall in the 2013 MLB draft by the Kansas City Royals, and he was dealt with the Athletics during the 2015 season.

He made his MLB debut in 2016, and on April 21, 2018, he threw a no-hitter. Before the 2022 season, the Athletics traded Manaea to the Padres.

How Much Is Sean Manaea Salary In 2022? His Net Worth Update

Sean Manaea has an annual salary of $5,950,000, with a $0 signing bonus, as of 2022. His entire net worth is estimated to be over $15 million.

Manaea agreed on a one-year contract with the Oakland Athletics worth $9,750,000 per year, with the same amount secured and an annual average salary of more than $9 million.

When the Oakland Athletics dealt the pitcher to the San Diego Padres on Sunday, they also got rid of his almost $10 million salaries.

They had already lowered their payroll to the point that they were towards the bottom of the league, thanks to a slew of top prospect losses and a few other star-for-prospects trades, and now they have cut it much further.

The salary was roughly $54 million before the Manaea trade, give or take based on which specifics he chose to add. It has already dropped to as low as $44 million.
