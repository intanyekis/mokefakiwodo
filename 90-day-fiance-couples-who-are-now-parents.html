'90 Day Fianc': 10 Couples Who Are Now Parents

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

As entertaining as 90 Day Fiance is, it's important to remember that the stars on the hit TLC show are real people. Critics have accused the show of being fake and staged. Granted, some 90 Day Fiancé moments were too ridiculous to ever be real, but there are also some very real moments on the show. Whatever the case may be, there's no denying that 90 Day Fiancé is a binge-able show and a guilty pleasure for most.

RELATED: How A Real Love Story Inspired 90 Day Fiance

Fans root for the brave men and women who decide to live their lives in front of the cameras for a shot at love, but they also appreciate the couples who are seemingly faking it—they always bring the drama. Most of the couples that genuinely found love on 90 Day Fiancé, got married, some of them had children and others are expecting.

10 Matt Ryan And Alla Fedoruk

Fans were introduced to Alla and Matt in the fourth season of 90 Day Fiancé, The pair met on a dating site and tied the knot in 2016. They were one of the couples featured on the show that fans were doubting but made it.

The couple welcomed a baby girl in 2020, it's their first child together. It's Alla's second child as she has a 12-year-old son named Max from a previous relationship.

9 Brett Otto and Daya De Arce

Like most 90 Day Fiancé couples, Brett and Daya met online. They were a fan favorite from the beginning and are among the most stable and drama-free couples on the show. Despite Brett's mother accusing Daya of being a gold digger and not supporting the relationship, the duo has been married for six years.

Brett has a preteen daughter from his previous marriage and he and Daya also share a daughter together. The reality star gave birth to their daughter Isabella in 2017.

8 Alexei And Loren Brovarnik

Loren and Alexei were featured on the third season of 90 Day Fiancé. Their story was beautiful and fans were taken by them. The pair met in Israel and they fell in love. Loren made a couple more trips back to Israel until Alexei could join her in the U.S.

They had quite a number of hurdles to cross and they did, they tied the knot in 2015. Loren and Alexei welcomed their son Shai Josef Brovarnik in 2020.

7 Amy And Danny Frishmuth

There have couples who came to 90 Day Fiancé for the wrong reasons, but the show has proved that love can be found in the unlikeliest of places. Danny and Amy made it look easy on season two of the show. They met in Australia and were smitten with each other.

Not everyone in Danny's family was supportive of the relationship because Amy is biracial. The pair worked through that and has been married for seven years and are parents to three adorable kids.

6 Ariel Weinberg And Biniyam Shibre

Ariel and Biniyam were on season two of 90 Day Fiance: The Other Way. They met while Ariela was vacationing in Biniyam's native country Ethiopia. The lovebirds learned they were expecting and Ariela decided to move to Ethiopia before the baby was born.

Ariela had a hard time adjusting to life in Ethiopia and questioned her decision to have their baby there. The pair welcomed their baby boy Aviel in 2019, who they are raising together.

5 Elizabeth Potthast And Andrei Castravet

Elizabeth and Andrei brought some much-needed drama to the fifth season of 90 Day Fiancé. The pair met in Dublin where Andrei was working at the time and fell in love, the lovebirds came under fire for expecting Elizabeth's father to finance them when Andrei relocated to the U.S.

From the get-go, 90 Day Fiancé viewers were critical of Andrei's treatment of Elizabeth, and her family butted heads with him. The pair who won some viewers over had two wedding ceremonies and welcomed their adorable daughter Eleanor in 2019.

4 Russ And Paola Mayfield

Paola and Russ are fan favorites of the 90 Day Fiancé franchise, viewers were introduced to the pair on the first season of the show. Their love story earned them both fans and critics, who accuse Paola of treating Russ poorly.

Eight years later, the couple is still married and even has a son together. The Mayfields had previously revealed that they'd suffered a miscarriage earlier on.

3 Karine Martins and Paul Staehle

Karine and Paul have made headlines for their marriage troubles, which have played out on TV. The couple was first featured on season one of 90 Day Fiancé: Before The 90 Days. They met online and Paul later traveled to Brazil to meet Karine in person.

It pretty much went downhill after that, the duo tied the knot despite their challenges and have since welcomed a son and have another child on the way.

2 Kirlyam And Alan Cox

It is safe to say that 90 Day Fiancé season one had the most successful relationships, Alan and Kirlyam are among the beloved cast of the first season. They met in Brazil years ago and were reunited later on. Alan and Kirlyam married in a Mormon temple and have stayed together ever since.

The duo hopped on the baby train in 2017 when they welcomed a baby boy named Liam. Fans have kept up with the reality stars on Instagram where they share cute family pictures.

1 Robert Springs And Anny Francisco

Robert proposed to Anny within eight hours of meeting her in person, the pair first met online in 2019. After the proposal, Anny traveled from the Dominican Republic to join her fiance in the U.S. Viewers met the lovebirds on season seven of the hit TLC show.

Robert was a father of one when he met Anny, and the two welcomed a daughter together in 2020 who they named Brenda Aaliyah.
