Cody Longo Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Cody Longo (born March 4, 1988) is an American film actor and musical artist. He currently stars as Eddie Duran on the Nick at Nite/TeenNick show Hollywood Heights.Longo played Andy Matthews in the remake of Fame, popular boy Evan Whitbourne in Bring It On: Fight to the Finish, Dave in Ball Don't Lie and Gavin Coley in Medium. In 2009 he appeared in an episode of Three Rivers.In 2010, he appeared in High School with Adrien Brody, Michael Chiklis as Chad and Piranha 3-D as Todd Dupree. He also appeared on the ABC show Brothers & Sisters.He appeared on NBC's Days of our Lives playing the role of Nicholas Alamain from August 23, 2011 to September 22, 2011.In 2012, Longo appeared in For the Love of Money with James Caan, Paul Sorvino and Jeffrey Tambor and will co-star in the thriller, The Silent Thief. among the roles he has played previously, his final outburst of fame was playing the character of Eddie Duran in the Nick series Hollywood Heights.Mirrorball Entertainment signed Cody Longo as a musical artist in 2012. Longo's debut single, Atmosphere, was released on August 22, 2012. The song hit the iTunes top 100 Pop chart on August 22, 2012.In the early morning of June 7, 2013, Longo was arrested on suspicion of driving under the influence. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
