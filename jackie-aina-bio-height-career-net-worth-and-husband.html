Age, Early life, and Family of Jackie Aina

On August 4, 1987, in the United States of America, Jackie Aina was born. Her real name is Jacquelyn Lonje Olayiwola Oyeshola Bolayemi Aina. She is 34 years old and a Leo by birth. Jackie was born to a Nigerian father who spoke Yoruba and an African-American mother.

She also has three additional siblings and two mothers, Folake Aina and Toyin Aina. Aina practiced medicine for two years in college before joining the US Army Reserve.
