Dorit Kemsley Biography: Husband, Age, Net Worth, Wikipedia, TV shows, Boyfriend, Children

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Dorit Kemsley, born Dorit Lemel on July 14, 1976, in Woodbridge, Connecticut, is an American fashion designer and television personality.

She has gained popularity as a main cast member on the reality TV show The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.

In addition to her television career, she is also the founder of the swimwear brand Beverly Beach by Dorit, which has garnered widespread acclaim in the fashion industry.

Early Life & Education

Dorit Kemsley, currently at 47, was born on July 14, 1976, in Woodbridge, Connecticut. She grew up in a Jewish family, with her father Shalom Lemel being Israeli and her mother Rachel hailing from Morocco.

Dorit attended Amity Regional High School in Woodbridge before pursuing her higher education.

After graduating from Quinnipiac University with a marketing, design, and communication degree, she embarked on a journey to Europe.

She settled in Italy for a decade and gained valuable experience working for a global swimwear company.

Personal Life

Dorit Kemsley is happily married to Paul Kemsley, and they have two children together, Jagger and Phoenix Kemsley.

The couple shares a strong bond and enjoys a fulfilling family life. They own a lavish 9,000-square-foot (840 m2) home in Encino, California, which they purchased for $6.47 million.

However, their peace was shattered in October 2021 when Dorit, along with her two children, was robbed at gunpoint by three male burglars at their Encino home.

Despite the terrifying ordeal, Dorit remains resilient and determined to overcome the incident.

Career

Dorit Kemsley’s career in the fashion industry has been remarkable. She launched her swimwear line, Beverly Beach, quickly gaining popularity among fashion enthusiasts.

Additionally, she ventured into the bridal collection market with her brand, Nektaria. Her creative designs and keen fashion sense have garnered praise and attention from critics and consumers alike.

Dorit’s breakthrough in the entertainment industry came when she joined the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills in 2016.

Since then, she has been an integral part of the show, appearing in a main capacity for six seasons. Her glamorous and confident persona has made her a fan favorite among viewers.

Net Worth

With her successful fashion ventures and television career, Dorit Kemsley has accumulated approximately $10 million net worth.

Her entrepreneurial spirit and talent have contributed to her financial success.
