Mike Conley Junior is an American basketball player who plays in the elite, National Basketball Association (NBA) for Utah Jazz. The player previously played for Memphis Grizzlies.

Michael Alex Conley Jr. popularly known as Mike Conley Jr. is an American professional basketball player who currently plays for Utah Jazz.

The player plays as the point guard for the Jazz. The professional basketballer has spent 12 seasons with the Grizzlies before being traded to Utah Jazz in 2019.

He is also all time leading scorer of the Memphis Grizzlies, scoring 11,733 points for them.

The basketball player has been an extraordinary athlete since his high school days. He and Greg Oden played together in both high school and college.

Mike Conley Jr. was born on October 11, 1987, in Fayetteville, Arkansas, United States.

The athlete gene seems to run in the Conley family, his father Mike Conley Sr. was an Olympian. He won gold and silver for track and field in the Olympics.

The Utah Jazz point guard went to Lawrence North High School in Indianapolis. He led his high school team to three consecutive championships.

He then went to Ohio State University. The player is managed by his father Mike Conley Sr. He signed with his father before his 2007 NBA draft.

The athlete has had a very good career, and will always be on the history book for the Grizzlies.

The athlete is on very good money with his contract earning him, $21,000,000 for the 2021/22 season, and rising to $22,680,000 and $24,360,000 for the 2022/23 and 2023/24 season respectively.

He is the third best-paid player in the Utah Jazz team, and the fifty-second best paid in the entire league.
