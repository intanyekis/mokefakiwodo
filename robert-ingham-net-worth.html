Australian businessman, Robert Ingham, has an estimated net worth of $745 million.

He is the sole shareholder of Australia's biggest poultry supplier and producer, Inghams Enterprises. However, his fortune was built from the sale of one of the country's largest racing empires, Woodland Stud Thoroughbred, in 2008. He continues to race a small number of horses. In the year 1953, after the death of his father, Walter Ingham, Robert Ingham and his brother Jack took over the small breeding operation and built it into the largest producer of chickens and turkeys in Australia. Headquartered in Sydney's Liverpool, the company has annual sales of more than $1.5 billion and a workforce of over six thousand people.
