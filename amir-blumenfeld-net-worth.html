Amir Blumenfeld is an Israeli-American comedian, actor, writer and TV host who has made his debut on screen in the 2011 comedy film titled A Very Harold and Kumar 3D Christmas. He also appeared in Hardly Working and co-starred in a web series titled Jake and Amir. He is a Hebrew literate and he uses his bilingual talent on some episodes of Jake and Amir. He also played a role on MTV's The College Humor Show and is presently working as the host of MTV's Pranked, together with Streeter Seidell, a College Humor employee.

Amir Blumenfeld is a graduate of University of California, Berkeley who currently works as a freelance contributor for the ESPN The Magazine in his spare time.
