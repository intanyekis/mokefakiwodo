Dave Howard Obituary, Family And Friends Mourn Dave Howard

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 30, 2024
0SHARESShareTweet

Dave Howard Obituary, Death – On Sunday, February 4, 2024, the Howard family bid farewell to their beloved patriarch, Donald David Howard, who passed away peacefully at the age of 72 in Nampa, Idaho. This tribute reflects on the remarkable life of a loving father, brother, and grandfather.

Early Years and Family Ties

Born on April 9, 1951, in Provo, Utah, to Juanita V. and L. Elsdon Howard, Dave was the second oldest of four children. His upbringing was marked by a close-knit family, and he shared a special bond with his siblings: brothers Ron Howard and Roy Howard, and sister Penny (Howard) Caldwell.

Dave’s Easygoing Nature

Known for his easygoing personality, Dave Howard embraced life with a passion for family, church, and scouting. These values shaped his character and influenced the lives of those around him. His warmth and approachability endeared him to family and friends alike.

 Union of Hearts

On January 4, 1974, Dave married the love of his life, Doreen Elizabeth Thomas, in a sacred ceremony at the Idaho Falls LDS Temple. Their union was blessed with three children: Doug (Meryn), Sean (Kathryn), and Scott. The Howard family became a testament to enduring love, shared joys, and a commitment to one another.

 Cherished Legacy

Dave Howard’s legacy extends through the lives of his surviving sons and 13 grandchildren. Doug (Meryn) Howard resides in San Tan, Arizona; Sean (Kathryn) Howard in Omaha, Nebraska; and Scott Howard in Nampa, Idaho. His grandchildren, undoubtedly touched by his wisdom and kindness, carry forward the torch of his legacy.

In Loving Memory

While celebrating Dave’s life, it is with heavy hearts that we acknowledge the loss of his beloved wife, Doreen Thomas Howard, his parents Juanita & Elsdon Howard, and his sister Penny (Howard) Caldwell. Their memories echo in the hearts of those who loved them, creating a tapestry of cherished moments.

Final Farewell

A funeral service to honor the life of Donald David Howard will take place at 11 a.m. on Saturday, February 10, 2024, at the Blackfoot Stake Center, 1650 Highland Dr., Blackfoot, Idaho. Following the service, interment will be held at the Groveland Cemetery. The gathering will serve as a moment to reflect on the impact of Dave’s life and to find solace in the memories shared by family and friends. In conclusion, Donald David Howard’s journey was one of love, commitment, and a legacy that transcends generations. As family and friends gather to bid their final farewell, they carry with them the indelible imprint of a life well-lived and a man deeply cherished.
