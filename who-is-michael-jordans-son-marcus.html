Who Is Michael Jordan's Son, Marcus?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 16, 2024
Read update
  • Marcus Jordan Has Had Quite The Experience Growing Up As Michael Jordan's Son

Marcus Jordan is the second child and youngest son of basketball legend Michael Jordan. The former college basketball player was born on December 24, 1990, to the basketball legend and his ex-wife Juanita Vanoy. Much like his father, Marcus played college basketball, but like his older brother, the shooting guard was never drafted to the NBA.

Although the former college basketball player did not follow in his father's footsteps in a professional basketball career, Marcus toed in the path of his father in opening his Jordan-inspired sneaker store. While Marcus seems like an open book, there are several layers to unravel about the former college basketball player, from his days in active sports to entrepreneurship, net worth, and his relationship status.

Marcus Jordan Has Had Quite The Experience Growing Up As Michael Jordan's Son

Marcus Jordan might have been born into basketball fame, but he's made his own headlines over the years, too, for everything from his basketball prowess to his self-made net worth.

10 Marcus Jordan Is One Of Five Jordan Kids

Marcus and his siblings Jeffrey and Jasmine were born to Michael Jordan's first wife. In 2014, Michael had two more kids (twin daughters) with his second wife, Yvette Prieto.

9 Does Marcus Jordan Play Basketball?

Marcus Jordan was an exceptional basketball player in high school, and his abilities in the sport made him an attractive option for colleges looking to recruit him for their team. Of the many schools on his tail, the basketball player decided to join the squad at UCF.

Marcus went with UCF because he wanted to play for a team that was interested in him for his skills rather than his father's name. During his time with The Knights, Marcus put up several impressive performances for three seasons before taking a bow from basketball.

Marcus' high school coach said of the player, "Marcus was looking for a place he could play, have an impact and feel comfortable on campus. The family felt there was no need to continue the recruiting process."

Another coach noted, "UCF made him feel comfortable. They wanted Marcus to come in to contribute right away and made him feel like he could make a big impact. They wanted Marcus for Marcus."

8 Marcus Jordan Studied Hospitality In College

Outside sports, the former college basketball player had other interests. Marcus Jordan majored in hospitality management to start a business. After Marcus quit basketball, he directed his focus entirely on his course of study.

Years ago, the entrepreneur revealed his inspiration to study hospitality management. Currently, his Twitter bio reads, "UCF Hospitality Management GRAD!"

7 Marcus Jordan Is A Business Owner

In 2016, Marcus Jordan's ambitions of owning a business were realized. The former college basketball player launched Trophy Room, a Jordan-inspired sneakers boutique modeled after Michael Jordan’s Trophy Room in the Jordan family home.

The Upscale retail store houses a collection of the rarest and most exclusive Air Jordans. The businessman also offers collaborations with top brands like Nike, and the Upper Deck Card Company, with a wide range of Nike and Jordan products for sale.

6 Marcus Jordan's Dating Life

Since the former student-athlete was spotted having dinner in Miami with Scottie Pippen’s ex-wife Larsa Pippen, there has been speculation about the relationship between the duo. These speculations were nearly laid to rest when Larsa dispelled the claims of any romantic involvement between them.

However, the two were later photographed holding hands, kissing, going to the beach, and even attending events together. The two finally seem to have admitted their relationship and have gone public with their PDA.

According to Us Magazine, the couple started stepping out in September 2022, despite Larsa having said she would try to avoid dating athletes, given her history with her ex-husband, also a basketball player. In October, Larsa claimed they were only good friends.

In February 2023, Larsa called Marcus her "forever valentine," and the two hit red carpets together in March 2023.

Only time will tell whether the relationship will last, of course.

5 What Is Marcus Jordan’s Net Worth?

The Trophy Room owner is incredibly loaded, and this is in no way due to Michael Jordan’s net worth. The former college basketball player has an estimated net worth of $1.5 million.

Marcus amassed his wealth from his short–term basketball career, coupled with his growing high-end sneaker boutique. Marcus’s bag might be getting even bigger with the expansion of his business to a physical location in Orlando.

4 Marcus Jordan Cost UCF An Adidas Deal

During Marcus Jordan's debut game for The Knights, Marcus made the biggest headline for his time with the UCF team. He donned a pair of Air Jordans to the preseason exhibition game for an Adidas-sponsored team. Marcus's actions caused Adidas to terminate the partnership, costing the university roughly $3 million in sponsorship money.

Years after the incident, the former student-athlete explained his side of the story, "one of the things they told me on my visit is that I was going to be able to wear Jordans, and it wouldn’t be a problem,” he shared with Complex.

Clearly, Marcus' family pride wasn't going to be overridden by his university's alliances, though that's up to fans to decide whether it was a good move or not.

3 Marcus' Tweet Sparked An Investigation Into A Nightclub

In 2010, Marcus Jordan allegedly caused difficulties for a nightclub in Los Angeles by boasting on Twitter about how much money he spent partying there. “Last night was stupid … 35K at Haze,” he wrote.

At the time of the tweet, Marcus was a minor (it's worth noting that he was born in 1990), ineligible to drink and gamble under Nevada state laws. The tweet, which has since been deleted, provoked an investigation into the operation of the nightclub.

2 Marcus Jordan Was Arrested For A Drunken Altercation

In 2012, Marcus Jordan was arrested in Omaha for disorderly conduct following an argument with two women in the hotel driveway in the wee hours of the morning.

After trying without success to restrain drunk Marcus, the hotel management called in the police to handle the situation. The Chicago native was arrested and charged with resisting arrest, driving while intoxicated, and obstructing, but he was released the same night.

1 His Mom Got Rich After Divorcing His Dad

Marcus' mom is Michael Jordan's first wife, Juanita Vanoy. Despite not having a prenup, Vanoy walked away from her divorce a very rich woman.

However, the exes remained amicable while raising their three kids.
