ABCs Nightline Interviews G. Dep On Confessing to Murder

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

Trevell Coleman, better known as G. Dep, is now speaking out about his sentencing for murdering a man 16 years ago.

The “Special Delivery” rapper was sentenced on May 8, 2012 and is serving 15 years to life in prison. ABC’s Deborah Roberts conducted a jailhouse interview with G. Dep who went into detail as he recounts the night he fatally robbed a stranger, confessing to the police in 2010, and exactly why he came forward as the gunman in the cold-case.

“I don’t feel like I’m cheating life too much anymore,” he said. “A lot of the burden is lifted, you know what I mean? And that was what I needed to do.”

Unbeknownst to his family members, the 39-year-old said he didn’t tell anyone of his actions because “I felt like I couldn’t really tell anybody. I didn’t want them to be involved.”

Watch the full interview tonight on ABC‘s Nightline at 12:35 a.m. ET.
