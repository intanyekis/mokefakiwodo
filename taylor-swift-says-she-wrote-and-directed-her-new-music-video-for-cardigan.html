Taylor Swift Says She Wrote And Directed Her New Music Video For 'Cardigan'

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 22, 2024

In the same exciting social media wave as the announcement of her new album Folklore, Taylor Swift also promised to release a music video to one of its new songs, Cardigan.

While she would have been touring for her previous album Lover, she made positive use of her time and shocked worldwide fans.

Swift tweeted out a photo from the music video that drops tonight, which appears whimsical and possibly even inspired by a fairy tale. The renowned singer shared that she directed and wrote the music video herself while thanking the other talented creatives involved in the shoot.

The tweet is already racking up likes and retweets as fans sit on the edge of their seats for this unexpected present. In a tweet following the initial album and music video announcement, Swift also shared that she did her own hair and makeup for the video.

Related: Fans Think Taylor Swift and Joe Alwyn Are Planning A Secret Wedding

Swift is sure to have a successful launch considering the accomplished team working with her and a sea of loyal listeners backing her up. The album Folklore will include fifteen songs plus a bonus track entitled The Lakes.

While the lyrics for Cardigan have yet to be released, Swifties are trying to discover any information they can, as the excitement is too much to handle.

The public is eager to learn what inspired Swift in writing this new album and will hopefully find some famous Easter eggs woven into her lyrics.
