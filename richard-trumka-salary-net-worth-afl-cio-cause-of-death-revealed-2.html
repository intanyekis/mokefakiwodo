Richard Louis Trumka was an American lawyer and labor organizer.

On September 16, 2009, he was elected president of the AFL-CIO during the federation’s conference in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

From 1995 to 2009, he was the AFL-secretary-treasurer, CIO’s, and from 1982 to 1995, he was the president of the United Mine Workers.

In 2011, Trumka was selected as an American of the Year by Esquire Magazine.

He graduated from Pennsylvania State University with a Bachelor of Science degree in 1971 and Villanova University School of Law with a Juris Doctorate in 1974.
